Desire

In fact, I have no idea how often you visit this site, or if you visit at all. But I don't care, because I want to improve it. Since this is the only salvation from non-communication with you. By adding anything to this site, I seem to communicate with you....

Not a day goes by that I don't think about you. I'm probably going crazy or already crazy. Now (08.09.2023) I am in Vegas and even then I wake up and go to bed thinking about you. So many questions and misunderstandings, it's like I'm in a maze. Also silence, not communication with you aggravates the situation. I really don't know what to do...

There will be many more pages on the site, and one of them is Desire. Here I would like to write all my Wishlist.

Most likely the very first is your response. I really want to correspond, call up with you, no matter what time. I will find time even when it is not, just to talk to you. I miss this so much.... When I wake up in the morning, the first thing I do is check notifications...

In your last letter, you wrote at the very end the phrase "thank you", I want and completely deny that these are words with a photo of your brother. I told you that I will fight for my love, and I will, but I don't know how yet. There is one famous person in the post-Soviet space and he got his wife after 8 years of struggle. He thought that he had no chance, because he knew that she was better than him. I have the same thoughts. I ask Dimana, do not say goodbye to me. Please. I just don't understand why I don't deserve at least one chance, just one chance, one....

Every single day I update this damn instagram, I can not focus on the objects and on the tour. 'Cause I'm waiting for something magical, a notification. Also, I don't want some of the rumors to be true....
