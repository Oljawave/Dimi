5 months, 12 days

I’m sitting here and thinking people say that if 5 months and 12 days are enough to forget a person. During this date, a fake person is forgotten, not the one who is needed. But if you continue to think for a long time, then this is a completely different conversation.

Yes, I’m not a philosopher and people say whatever they want. But if you still read this page, then almost six months have passed since August. Does this say anything? I can not understand. The first page was created on 27.08.2023

Even though I always have headphones in my ears, I listen to different music, sometimes classical, sometimes pop. But I don’t know why, but the spiderman album from the metro reminds me of you most of all. It may sound absurd, but it’s as if the music charges me and I become more charged, as if I’m about to fly. But when I listen to Travis, it’s like I’m selfish, it’s a pity that I wasn’t raised like that.

They say God tests us with those we love most, perhaps this is happening right now, that he tests us, well, me in particular. Back in the USA, I bought a crystal necklace in a gold mining town in Arizona, so that I would give it to you later. I honestly expected such a response from you when I invited you to Dubai, it was like “Elementary, my dear Watson”. I thought I'd give it there. Russians say hope dies last, and hope will die with me.
