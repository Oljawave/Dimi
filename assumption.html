In your eyes

October 2, 2023

Perhaps, in your eyes, I am not the person you would like your partner to be. Maybe it's because I don't live up to certain standards or expectations. And perhaps this raises doubts about whether I can provide a stable future. These thoughts torment me, and I begin to wonder: what if this has something to do with my personality or physical characteristics?

Asian, Muslim, Kazakh - all these labels seem to become an obstacle, forcing you to doubt yourself. Maybe I really am not as attractive or confident as I would like. Maybe my fears and insecurities make me too caring and boring in your eyes.

But despite all these thoughts, I want to understand and be understood. I strive to become a better person, to be strong and confident enough to create a bright and promising future.

Although I am Muslim, I am not an Arab or a religious fanatic. Although I am a Kazakh, I am not an Uzbek, a Mongol, or even a two-faced Turk

To love but not to be loved

October 2, 2023

Perhaps God created me to be a giver of love, but not to be loved. I am not one who considers myself a romantic, but a candle of love burns in my heart. God gave me this gift, and I feel that I know how to see the beauty in people, I know how to love and accept them as they are.

Perhaps this is my purpose - to be someone who brings joy and warmth into the lives of others. I don't know how to hold a grudge, and I don't try to change my natural gifts. I find my happiness in love and acceptance, and if my essence is called to be just like that, then there is no way I can escape it
