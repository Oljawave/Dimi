Extraordinary dream

Hey.....l
I had a dream again, but this time it was special, long and unusually clear. Somehow we both ended up abroad. This was not an accident, but a deliberate step. The dream began with a dance - we danced in the cozy living room of a house. They danced for a long time, talked, hugged, and laughed. You suddenly said: “Let’s go for a walk, let’s go to a cafe.” I took the money, the keys, and we, happy, went outside.
It was getting evening when we walked hand in hand, conducting an endless dialogue on a variety of topics. I tried to make you laugh, and every time I saw you smile, I felt the same feelings as in the summer in the USA. Walking in the park and on the street, you suggested going to a cafe. Turning towards the cafe, we noticed some kind of disorder near the building. A man in a wheelchair screamed and threw stones.
Suddenly one of the bricks flew towards us. I instantly covered you, protecting you from danger. The man continued to throw himself at passers-by, and then I suddenly woke up, feeling pain and not immediately realizing reality. It was 4:45 am.
This dream left behind a strange feeling. The whole situation was so vivid, as if it had actually happened. I realized again how much you mean to me, being there and seeing your smile every day. This dream reminded me that real feelings do not disappear over time, but rather only intensify. I realized that I want to create such moments in reality - to travel, dance, laugh and protect you from all the storms that life can send.
