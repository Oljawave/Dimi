17.08

What else

Salem Dimana, think u good
I'm good. Getting ready to go to Astana, to begin new chapter of my life, if I can say like this. Cause there is nothing new hahaha. But anyways. Too much work. My mind is always at work, but right now, it feels like it's working overtime. You know, when things got really tough last summer, the only solution I found was you. Only you could bring me peace, which is why I long so much to return to you. Sometimes I get very tired, as if I want to lay my head on your knees and fall asleep. Although it is worth understanding that this is impossible.
Five days in the office, weekends at the store, and nights spent on projects. This is my cycle, and there's no escaping it. But sometimes, there comes a moment when I'm so mentally exhausted that I just need to get away. I grab a milkshake or something tasty, head to a park or the Expo, sit on a bench, turn on Tame Impala or The Neighbourhood, and just watch life go by. Birds, tourists, passing faces—everything becomes a backdrop for my thoughts, moments of quiet that I can only find in these brief respites.
There are days when I simply look through all your photos from that summer. With "SOS" by the little-known artist INNR CIRCLE playing in the background, I freeze, mesmerized by your image. How can you be so beautiful? A whole year has passed, yet you still mean more to me than the entire world. No one understands me, not even you. I just want to hold you again, to feel your presence even for a moment, to watch you, to observe your quirks. This feeling of longing—it's so heavy, so hard to bear. Why the life is so unfair...
When people grow up in tough conditions, they often develop low self-esteem. I always kept people at a distance, believing that if I didn’t let anyone get close, no one could hurt me. It became my protective armor. But when we started talking, I realized how much I had missed human warmth. With you, for the first time in my life, I didn’t feel alone. You opened my heart.
Now, with you far away, I understand how deeply you've intertwined with my life and thoughts. I search for your smile among the faces of strangers, catch echoes of your voice in the city's noise. Every little thing associated with you becomes a reminder of what I’ve lost and how much I miss you. Sometimes I imagine us together again, just holding you, feeling the warmth of your presence. It's such a simple wish, yet it feels so distant, unattainable.
Everyone knows how Napoleon loved Josephine, and one day, sitting in the sand of Egypt, looking into the distance, he said, "Do you think about me for even a second?"
