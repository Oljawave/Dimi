9.05

Dream

Hey, i think all good with you.
Everything is fine with me, I arrived in Astana. I have a diploma project. Almost finished, just a little left. We are currently working on this with the guys. I think we'll finish soon. I was thinking I didn’t introduce you to the city. Below I will leave some photos with a description. In general, I would like to invite you to Kazakhstan, just for my graduation. Of course, if you don’t have any plans for the summer. The city of Astana is the youngest capital of the world, there are a lot of architectural buildings, for example, the largest tent in the world, the tallest building in Central Asia and Europe (not counting Russia) and much more. But the coolest thing here is the sunsets. Sunset in Astana is the most beautiful I have seen in the world. Even the sunset in Los Angeles is not close. I would really like to show all this to you. Although I live in Kazakhstan, I haven’t been to many places, for example, in the resort of Aktau, in the megacities of Almaty and Shymkent, in the Alatau mountains and in the deserts of Mangistau. But I won't lie, I don't want to get rejected again. For boys, getting rejected is the worst thing that can happen, and I’m also afraid. It's like burning your hand on a kettle, the second time you won't touch it, but you want to. And by writing here, I simply won’t even know whether you read it or not, thus it won’t hurt me. And that's why I think it will be better. The presentation of the diploma will be held in the “Library of the First President” in Astana. My sick imagination imagines how I receive my diploma and at the same moment I see you waving in the distance. Yes, yes, I love to dream. But still, I don’t know the exact date yet, but it will be somewhere between 26.06 - 01.07. And then I would show you all the beauty of my native land, Astana simply makes you fall in love with its beauty:
