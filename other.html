Before meeting her, I was a completely different person, only with her arrival in my life did I begin to truly smile. Everyone will say that I went crazy for loving a foreigner, but only she managed to bring me back as a person. Many may say that she was not the one, you simply idealized her because she devoted time to you and you became attached, but no... because I know what I’m talking about. And she also knows and understands. She said that distance was not a barrier, but deep down I understood that she was still afraid. But I didn’t know how to explain to her that distance means nothing when someone means everything. That there is no need to be afraid of distance, because you can love far away, and you can break up close.
