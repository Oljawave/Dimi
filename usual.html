16.06

Another dream

Hey Dimana, hope u r okay
From the news I defended my thesis. The topic of the thesis was the development of a platform for automating parking spaces in Kazakhstan, especially in the large cities of Astana and Almaty. You have no idea how difficult it is to find a parking space on busy days in Astana. And I got 90 points! Can you imagine? The commission was complicated, tbh, they were all Koreans and Indians. I'm glad everything went smoothly. Now all that remains is to get a diploma and that’s it.
Dream again. This time the dream began in the park, at a picnic. Everything was so vivid and realistic that it seemed as if I was reliving that very day again. The park resembled a botanical garden in Astana, but that didn’t matter. It all started with laughter. We told each other funny stories and watched the children playing.
Suddenly you said that you want to tell me something. I, of course, agreed. You started talking, complaining and scared at times. Since it was a dream, I don't remember all the details and dialogue. But I clearly remember you talking about some tall, thin guy with long hair. I didn’t know what was so scary about him, but you described his actions with obvious concern.
After that, you suddenly hugged me, and I felt your tears rolling down my cheek. You whispered in my ear: “Save me.” At that moment I woke up.
The dream itself was very strange because you spoke in Russian and I have no idea who this person you were talking about was. Initially I didn’t want to write about this dream, but then I thought that maybe I had a dream for a reason.
Прошу тебя, следи за собой, будь осторожной и береги себя. Знаю, что мне уже нет места в твоей жизни, но остается только молиться Богу, чтобы у тебя всё было хорошо.
