She deserves more, a better person and simply superior to me. And I, and I just needed her, she is my wings. She is my angel, who often pulled me out of the bottom of my dark days. She played such an important role in driving death away from me, she opened my eyes and was a guide. She pulled me onto the high road and then, and then I don’t know what to do, I won’t be able to move and now, looking forward, I realize that I can move on only with her, because she is my constant support and the meaning of my existence.

Why? Because only this person managed to light me up after complete devastation. I don’t know how God sent me there and met me with her. After panic attacks, forgetting what life is, wanting only death, I lost my meaning and desire. And she returned it all, that's why she is the meaning

I realize that actions speak louder than words and a guy is judged by his actions. However, give me a chance, and I will show not just with words, but with my actions what it means to be a real man.
