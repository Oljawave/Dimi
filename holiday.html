22.03

Nauryz

Hello, I hope you are doing great. Today is our New Year. In other words, Nauryz. The most important holiday in the Turkic states. It was very beautiful in the cities. I came to celebrate with my family. But I want to leave already, it’s somehow unbearable to be in the same house with my father. Very unusual. But oh well. In the morning I was in Astana, and came to Karaganda again. He has grown and changed a lot. And the celebrations were at the highest level. The truth is, every time I see something beautiful, I want you to see it too. Every time I see something beautiful, my desire for your presence arises like an invisible magnet drawn to you. I want to share this beauty with you, as if every moment becomes even more precious when I know that you can enjoy it too. Yes, my subconscious always draws up places and events where you and I, while traveling, see the most beautiful places on the planet. I don’t know, but for me travel is a priority. Perhaps daydreaming. Dozens of countries and hundreds of beautiful places. Ты и я...
