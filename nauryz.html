Nauryz

i like being alone
but that is why i knew you were different
because for the first time ever
i wanted someone else's company
more than my own

Magic word

Today is Thanksgiving Day in Kazakhstan, it is different from Thanksgiving Day in the West. After all, we associate it with warmth and the onset of spring; the month of March (Nauryz) is considered the beginning of the year among nomads, a month of blessings, fun and happiness.

I deliberately did not create pages with the preposition “Thank you,” because I began to associate it with the word “bye.” But I think it’s on this day that I can write about it, without a hint of “bye.” It’s a pity that the text cannot convey the intonation with which I say this. But let's agree that you will read this without despondency and tears (perhaps). Only a smile is accepted.

I want to thank you for being able to pull me out of the hole into the light, showing me that there is a continuation in this wonderful world. Thank you for my smile, for my laughter and mood in the mornings of a wonderful summer. Thank you for the emotions you gave me. Thank you for giving me the opportunity to feel capable. I am very grateful for making it clear that I am capable of loving so much. Thank you for the most beautiful views I have ever seen. Thank you for lighting up my heart, brain, and all 150 other feelings and abilities. Thank you for giving me something to think about. Thank you for giving me hope and faith in something that I thought was impossible. Thank you for your trust, yes for your trust. Thank you for spending time with me. Yes, I’m pathetic, maybe not very nice, but thank you for spending time with me, even at night after work. Thank you for showing me what I can do for the sake of someone who is truly dear to me. Thank you for making me feel multitasking and far-sighted like Napaleon, strong and fearless like Atilla, thoughtful and catastrophically doubtful like Poirot, passionate and crazy, a person who knows how to love like Sherlock. Thank you to your mother for giving life to a wonderful person like you.

I don’t understand people who start saying bad things about people with whom things didn’t work out. Just so you know, I don’t believe and will never believe a single bad word about you, but on the contrary, I pray for you every Friday. I am the complete opposite of everything in the world, maybe that’s what makes me so strange. I simply believe that compassion makes people kinder and the world a better place.

But so far everything is going wrong. I lost everyone and even Aldiyar (he left me, we haven’t communicated since the moment we arrived, otherwise it doesn’t matter) with whom it was difficult to imagine my next step. There is no one left, well, okay, we’ll live like that. It's not for me to judge everyone. In general, probably no one cares, otherwise at least I’m not Joni from Starbucks ahahahahaha, let’s end on this note. Good night.
