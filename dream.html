Once in a while

Heeyy. Hope you are fine.

I almost never sleep during the day, only at night. But today, when I came home from university, I suddenly fell asleep. The most interesting thing is that I have dreams 2-3 times a year, very rarely. And just today I had a dream. In a dream, I’m in a hurry somewhere with my backpack and a black T-shirt. Everything was so unclear, I was still in hyperlapse, running somewhere, constantly looking at my watch, getting on the bus, getting off, running and running. As a result, I find myself on some street, it’s hot outside, I’m in a hustle and bustle. Then I open some kind of door, it seems like it was red. And there you are... I see you and this hyperlapse focuses on the eyes, the rest is all in blur. You're wearing the same "Molly Ringworm" T-shirt, looking up at me, and your look was so sad but at the same time joyful. And you hugged me and said, “Why did you take so long, did I really have to wait so long, I thought that you wouldn’t come...” And I see myself overly pleased and happy, as if everything was supposed to happen. (I saw everything in third person). Then the hyperlapse scenes again with a small FPS and rays of the sun, I say something funny and you laugh a lot. Walking around the city, someone calls me all the time, and I hang up and hang up. Then I asked you for forgiveness and picked up the phone, and in the end I found myself on the floor with the phone where my friend was calling me. Afterwards it took me a while to realize that it was a dream. And so I’m writing about it here, perhaps this is not a coincidence or I’m again doing everything very colorfully

May your days be filled with boundless happiness and warmth. In a world where sorrow can burden the heart, a simple smile can work wonders, lifting spirits and spreading joy far and wide. Embrace the power of positivity, letting it radiate from your being and illuminate the world around you. So, keep on smiling, for your smile is a beacon of light in the darkness. Though I may not witness it directly, know that your happiness brings me joy from afar, warming my soul and filling me with delight.

Колко много хора, с които можеш да поговориш,
колко малко с тези, с които искаш да се събудиш
и да ги видиш с усмивка на лицето сутрин,
и целия ден да чакаш с нетърпение новини.


Колко много хора, с които можеш просто да живееш,
но ти търси с кого искаш да сънуваш,
да гледаш как облаците плават по небето,
да пишеш любовни думи на първия сняг,
и да не познаваш по-голямо щастие или желание.


Ти търси с кого можеш да мълчиш,
който разбира думите наполовина,
