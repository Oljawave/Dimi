Dear Dimana,

I use your thought to write this letter. I have never written letters before, much less websites. The site may seem ridiculous, but why not.

I lived my whole life as a North Korean before I met you. You have become the key to my world, you have opened a lot in me. But as I said before, this door cannot always be open. After all, the door always needs a key.

You called me a bright star, but by calling me that you had no idea that you served as Thor Odinson, who lit Nedavelir. You said I should believe in myself first, but I've always believed in myself, not my actions. But you made me believe in my actions too.

When I called you a phoenix, I didn't mean mythology. I meant how bright you are, and how you respond to my dark world by being around. You want to fly alone for a while, I can't be against it, but let me fly after you. After all, you also believe that I can fly too. I don't want to lose sight of you.

We don't have to be a couple right now, but give us a chance to do it, that's all I ask.

Sincerely yours, Sherlock
