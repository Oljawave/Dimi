25.03

Nauryz

Hello, I hope you are okay)
Today I had a dream again. There you were, so beautiful in a dark green dress. I don’t remember almost anything anymore, maybe when I remember I’ll add this page
I tell myself that our story is over, but every time someone mentions your name or just asks about you, my heart skips a beat. I still remember every moment I spent with you. Every day I ask myself thousands of questions. I still see you in my dreams and feel your presence, but every day I forget your laughter. Yes, I am not a cool, daring and far from desirable guy and never will be. I couldn't give you anything, but for the first time I was able to feel happy. I believed in these words very much “if u really love that person, learn to wait. “maybe you’re not meant to be together today, but meant to be in the future” but every day I doubt it more and more. But still, seeing something cute on the street, someone who is hugging, simply rejoicing, children who are having fun, kissing their parents and everything like that, you and only you immediately come to mind, and I smile with the thought that I also had such a feeling
