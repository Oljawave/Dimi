10.03

Ramadan

Hello. I hope that everything is fine with you and that everything is going as you planned. Everything is the same for me, everything seems to be fine. Ramadan is over, and that means we can eat. This post flew by like a rocket. And it wasn’t hard at all, as if I hadn’t eaten from dawn to dusk all my life. I came to Karaganda again, almost every 2 weeks I come here. Why? Because I opened a store here. Don't tell anyone about this, it's a secret. Yes, remember I said that I wanted to invest my money somewhere, so I decided to open a store. Of course, after I tested it in Astana. In the capital, the project was launched successfully, but it is not mine, I was just an accomplice, and now I am the owner. I have an auto tuning shop. Now I'm in Karaganda, setting up my point. I do all sorts of advertising on Instagram and TikTok. After the store gains momentum, I will keep it as passive income. The demand is great, I was surprised when I saw my hometown from the inside, since when I left here the city was not so voluminous. Now the city has become very large, the population has increased to 1 million people, new areas are being built, every day I pass these new areas and parks and just get lost in the music in my ears. And then I think, what am I even doing? Is what I'm doing now correct? Am I wasting my time? Yes, the store is alive and I am making a profit, but for some reason these questions do not disappear. On the one hand, I have a bunch of projects related to professions and then business. It's like I'm trying to sit on two chairs. Okay, let's see what comes of this.
This is the month of grace, they say that whatever you ask from God in this suspended month, it will definitely come true. All 30 days I asked for your safety, so let him always protect you.
я скучаю, наверное ты не заходишь сюда, но мне плевать, я буду продолжать, потому что это единственное место где я могу выражаться как хочу
