24.09

Beautiful date

No, I haven't forgotten about your birthday! How can you forget such an important day, when the world becomes a little brighter because you appeared. I want to congratulate you with all my heart and wish that every new day brings you joy, success and confidence that everything in this life is going according to your scenario.
I won't congratulate you personally, because you probably already have that one person from whom you really expect congratulations. But that doesn't mean I don't remember and don't want to wish you all the best. May this day be filled with warmth, care and joy, and may the one who is dear to you give you the very feeling that you deserve.
Happy Birthday, Dimana.
