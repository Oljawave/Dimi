05.03.2024

Heyy
It so happened that today I finished my bachelor's degree. Not officially of course, but I passed my last session in my bachelor's degree. Now all that remains is to defend my diploma, and this will be in the summer. It’s somehow strange and extraordinary that at just 20 years old I graduate from university. In my first year I was 17 and I thought that my whole university life was still ahead, but look it was already over. I'm at a loss. Will I miss uni? No. Rather, I will miss the time, being a student, my projects and the excitement of mathematics. It’s very old, it’s strange to realize that I need to move on. Achieve what I wanted. I graduated as a programmer. I like it so much. But sometimes it seems that I am a politician, hahahaha. I like to analyze every detail and be clear. I also love history and geography. Sometimes it seems that I was born for these paths. Yes, sometimes I look at our president and I can be seen in his manners, hahahaha. But my journey with Astana IT University is almost complete. This page is more like that, neutral. It might be interesting to look at some photographs from the university.

“We often don’t say in time I miss you to those who should.” I heard this phrase today while driving home on the bus. This was an elderly grandmother. And I really wondered if I say this often or not? What will this help? Why is this phrase true? And I realized that I wrote here about this almost always.
