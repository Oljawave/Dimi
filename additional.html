Fear | Pray | Wait

There is only one picture in my head that scares me. This is me in the winter in a cold jacket with a hood with headphones in my ears, 7 pm in the dark, I’m walking home alone. This happened to me last year; while describing this situation, I also described my inner world.

In my culture, the patient gets what he wants. There are many legends about this. Only a patient person who knows how to wait will get what he deserves in the end. So I'll wait, I'll wait. You once asked me if it would take several years. I said I don’t care if it leads to me, I’m ready to wait for an eternity. Those who know how to wait will wait for more.

I’d rather go through all these troubles, even if I fall, get up right away, and finally get to you and live my whole life with you, rather than spend it on someone with whom I don’t want to be. You give me so much happiness without even knowing it, and I am ready to give you everything.

The only thing I can do now is pray, ask God and wait. If a person knows how to love, then he must be able to wait. As you said, if you love a person, let him go, and he will definitely return. You believe in it, and I will wait...

Don’t think that all these are just words and that it will go away over time. No... I will always wait for that very message from the very girl for whom I am ready to do anything. My flame will never go out for you Dimana....

Я скучаю по тебе...
