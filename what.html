"Love is a dagger, so beautiful but so sharp"
Loki Laufeyson

I kept wanting and wanting to be someone who could be like everyone else, interesting, loved and simply a good person. Unfortunately, I didn't. There must be an explanation for that, because I still go to the movies alone. I did everything, I went against my principles for you. No, no, I'm not blaming you or holding a grudge or trying to evoke pity. Just everything I did wasn't enough, or something in me wasn't enough, that's just fact, there's no other way to explain it. Back when we were in the USA, you wanted to stop communicating because I didn't give you a choice. I kept thinking they misunderstood you, but now I think I should probably leave you alone. This is so hard to write.

I wanted so much to hear the words I love you, to hear that I'm important to someone, I was hoping to hear it from you. But I said “I love you” not out of a desire to hear the same in response, I just wanted to make sure that I shed a light of awareness on you. I wanted too much and I guess my hour didn't come. I guess I'm just a boy with a childhood trauma that was forgotten around you. It was only with you that I found peace. I loved you truly, that's what I'm 100 percent sure of. And even the dragonflies flying into Starbucks proved it to me.
