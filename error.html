Hi again

Hey, I hope u well
I haven't made any updates for a very long time. A lot of interesting things happened this month. I finished my project, completed my bachelor's, got my driver's license and much more. Probably only you can find out about everything and only on this site, since I don’t like Instagram. Well, I don’t know, I just don’t like him, I recently found out that it turns out that there is something like “best friends”, so this is generally a mess. But okay.
Yes, I finished my studies, I’m 20 years old. I wonder if I’m already 20 or just 20. You know, that period of life has probably come when a guy should become a man. Don’t suffer through bullshit, but do really worthy things. I don’t know how it is in your homeland, but here a boy has to provide for his parents, then start his own family, provide for her too, please his wife, build a house for his parents, plant a tree and all that. To be honest, should be making a ton of money.
Every time I ask girls for something, I still say your name to them. But I don’t think they understand - the name Diman simply does not exist for them here in Kazakhstan. How can I miss you for so long and so much? This damn iPhone, like a faithful keeper of memories, doesn’t let me forget you, throwing endless photos of our love at me on the screen. Even if it hurts I like it.
And I don't know what to do with this sadness. It's like a fog envelops me, not allowing me to breathe. I miss your laughter, your touch, the way you look at me. I’ve probably already forgotten some little things, but your smell cannot be erased from my memory. He, like a ghost, haunts me everywhere, awakening the brightest memories in my soul. Even in the crowd I look for your face, hoping for a miracle that one day you will be near me again.
