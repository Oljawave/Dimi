Looking at the stars, I see only you...

Starry night

Beneath a celestial tapestry of a thousand sparkling stars, as I glance up at the endless expanse of the night sky, I find myself in awe of a strange, radiant presence. It is you, who decorate my thoughts and fill my heart with an unearthly radiance.

In your eyes I discover constellations of secrets and galaxies of untold stories waiting to be explored. Your smile outshines the gentle glow of the moon, and in the soft whisper of the night I seem to hear the voice of your laughter, and your angelic voice which will never be enough.

When I look at the stars, I am reminded that you are the most beautiful constellation in my universe, the North Star guiding me through the darkest nights, and the warmth of the distant sun bathing my world in eternal radiance. My heart, my everything - forever, it is you I see among the stars.

Dream & Savior

I remember vividly when you told me that you can't tether your happiness to a person, and I boldly declared that I would defy that wisdom. For in the depths of my heart, I held an unwavering faith in that person, unlike any other.

You spoke of the dream you had, and in that moment, I was gripped by a potent mixture of fear and astonishment. The raw honesty of your revelation left me momentarily speechless.Because this is the honest truth, and I was amazed that you saw it. As if I also saw this dream. The wind plays with the curtains, all the details are white, I sleep on the bed, next to me there is a knife, and of the people who can help me, only you... In fact, you always saved me during that period and I still see you as a savior. I also called you a phoenix because in some legends it is a symbol of salvation. And I’m still shocked by this, because this dream didn’t happen to you in vain...
