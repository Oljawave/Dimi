Moments

memories that will remain in my head forever and will not be erased under any circumstances

You and I were standing at that moment when I decided to take the first step and start a conversation. I took a deep breath and said my first words in Bulgarian: “Здравей, как беше денят?” Your eyes lit up with slight surprise, and at that moment I felt my heart beating faster.

The day we entered the store will remain in my memory forever. You noticed the beautiful earrings, and without a moment’s doubt, I instantly ran back and bought them. Your joy, and of course the first hug, became a real treasure for me.

One day I showed you a Kazakh shapan, you put it on and walked all the way to the house. I still remember how all the Kazakhs looked at you with amazement and admiration. The color of the shapan was emerald and it went so well with you.

The moment I truly felt useful. It's not a funny moment, but I'm glad I helped with the bag full of wet clothes. Then you said if I don’t give you the package, then you will stop talking to me, at that moment I realized that you also understand how dear a conversation with you is to me )
