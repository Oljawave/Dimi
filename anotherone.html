25.07

Time

Hi.
I decided to make another update while I have the time. I'm doing well. Home, work, home. That's how it is these days. I still go to Karaganda. A funny thing happened. Here, people call me Olzhik, and sometimes Jassik. And my mom calls me Justin. We both love Timberlake. I don't know, we just liked how he sings and his movies. One day, we went to the supermarket with my mom. She shouted, "Timberlake." So I approached her, and I noticed that people around us didn't understand and thought my name was really Timberlake.
I don't want to work fully in my profession yet. I don't know, I want something of my own. You know, university teaches us to be slaves, teaches us how to work for those who dared to take risks. So why shouldn't I take a risk? I want to start a business to leave something for my future kids. I don't wanna be like my father. Now I'm struggling by myself, without any support and communication. Maybe it is to become a man. This summer, I think I understood what it means to be confident. It turns out that confidence is knowing exactly what you want and also having money. No, I'm not from a wealthy family, just middle class. But working, I became more confident.
For now, I don't see anything other than work and travel. There are too many things I want in this life. I'll be doing that. Honestly, I don't know why I'm writing all this here, it's easier to write in Word, anyway, you don't come here anymore. And yet, I keep writing here like a fool. Oh no, I know why, because in 3 months you became everything to me, but I'm just a passerby to you I guess. Yes, it's kind of sad, but what should i do.
